About Me

Mr. Johan is a seasoned entrepreneur and professional forex trader with over a decade of experience in financial markets. He began his journey in 2012, mastering forex trading strategies, market analysis, and risk management techniques. His expertise has enabled him to consistently generate profitable outcomes while minimizing risk exposure. Beyond forex, Mr. Johan has established successful traditional businesses in the gold and oil sectors, gaining deep insight into commodity markets, supply chains, and international trade. This diversified experience allows him to combine traditional business acumen with modern financial technologies. In 2025, he founded Ai Forex, an innovative platform designed to leverage AI in financial trading, aiming to create a decentralized, intelligent trading ecosystem. His vision extends to integrating multiple projects under the Ai Forex umbrella, including e-commerce, real estate, import-export, agent banking, online banking, and crypto debit solutions, reflecting his commitment to building a robust, future-ready financial ecosystem. Mr. Johan is committed to bringing premium, secure, and transparent financial solutions to clients globally, driven by innovation, discipline, and a long-term vision for growth.
